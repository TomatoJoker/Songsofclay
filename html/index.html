About the Artist

Living in Italy since 1996, the Ukrainian sculptor, Hevorga Noci, graduated from the School of Fine Arts Manuel Belgrano and majored in Sculptor in the Superior School of Fine Arts in Santa Rosaria, Italy.

In parallel to the work as a fine artist and researcher of folk Ukrainian instruments, she has been intensively working with clay and ceramic materials. Hevorga participated in workshops, exhibitions and festivals related with porcelain art in Italy and the world.
